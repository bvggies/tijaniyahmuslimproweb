What We Accept

​The Muslim Pro brand is positive, uplifting and aims to build a global Muslim community through shared values. Content created must have a Muslim-centric angle that can either educate, entertain or inform our audiences.

Eager to share about Ramadan in your country? Do you want to do a movie review of a show from Qalbox? Have any photographs, halal food recommendations, personal stories or travel tips to share? Crafting a useful infographic for fellow Muslims to use? Let’s hear it!

We welcome contributions under these categories:

  • Deen ⏤ Quran, Islamic, Faith-Based, etc. 
  • Technology ⏤ Digital Literacy, Innovation in Islam, etc
  • Lifestyle ⏤ Qalbox, Travel, Recipes, Parenting, etc.
  • Community ⏤ Personal Stories, Ramadan Overseas, etc

There are multiple sub-topics under each category as the Muslim Pro Blog is a dynamic content hub for writers and creatives to showcase their talents and perspectives.

We extend a warm invitation to writers, bloggers and Muslim Pro users to contribute to Muslim Pro’s official blog.

We are actively seeking out fresh perspectives and viewpoints from talented creatives. No matter your writing experience, we value your contribution and creative ideas. Join our community of passionate creatives and let your work be seen globally.

Simply submit your story through this form.

We review all submissions, but due to the high volume, it may take a bit of time to get back to you. Our editorial team will carefully assess your submission and provide a response as soon as possible. If you haven't heard from us within a month, unfortunately, your submission wasn't accepted this time. Don't worry, though; we'll keep you informed throughout the process via email.

To ensure your submission meets our publication standards, it might undergo a few rounds of revisions. We'll work closely with you during this process to make sure everything aligns. We appreciate your patience and understanding!

For more details, please check out our Community Guidelines.
