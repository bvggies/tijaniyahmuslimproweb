Hope For Palestine 2025 | Letter from Nafees, Muslim Pro CEO

  • Muslim Pro Contributor
  • 28 September 2025
  • CommunityMuslim Pro App
بِسْمِ اللهِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِيْمِ
In the name of God, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful.

Assalamualaikum Muslim Pro community,

As Muslim Pro turns 16, I am reminded that our journey has always been more than technology. From the beginning, we sought to build a community — one that nurtures faith, inspires hearts, and strengthens connections across the world.

That community is you. Every time you open the app to check prayer times, read Qur’an, or make a du’a, you are part of the Muslim Pro community, united in faith and purpose.

This year, we dedicate our 16th anniversary to Hope for Palestine. This October, your daily faith habits on the Muslim Pro app will go beyond your screen. Each pledge, each prayer, each act of worship will contribute to real aid for Palestinian families in need.

Here is how you can be part of this campaign:

  1. Join the Community Challenge: Take part in meaningful quests each day — reading Qur’an, joining livestreams, making dhikr, journaling your reflections, asking questions on AiDeen, and more. With every completed quest, you’ll earn stars. At the end of the Challenge, Muslim Pro will transform those stars into tangible aid for Palestinian families. Keep an eye out for our latest app update so you’ll be notified once the Challenge starts!
  2. Learn About Palestine: Throughout the month, explore a rich line-up of content and live sessions with renowned scholars and experts. Discover stories of resilience, unity, and hope, and deepen your understanding of Palestine.
  3. Hope for Palestine Charity Drive: Contribute directly through the app by purchasing food packs and other essentials. With the help of trusted partners like Smart Qurban International, your support will reach families in Gaza and those who have been displaced, bringing relief where it is needed most.
  4. Share Your Artwork for Palestine: Express your solidarity through art. Submit your Palestine-inspired art — selected works will be featured across Muslim Pro platforms as part of a collective voice of resilience and hope.

Remember to update your app to take part in events. Together, our faith can inspire action, and our action can create hope.

Sixteen years on, our mission remains unchanged: to serve and uplift the global Muslim community. This year, we do so by standing with Palestine.

From our community to theirs, from your hands to their hearts — may Allah ﷻ accept these efforts and make them a source of hope and dignity.

With gratitude,
Nafees Khundker
CEO, Muslim Pro
